# Banking Methodology

The application implements a simplified version of the following banking concepts:

- IFRS 9 Expected Credit Loss (ECL)
- Probability of Default (PD)
- Loss Given Default (LGD)
- Exposure at Default (EAD)
- IFRS 9 Stage Classification
- Internal Credit Risk Grading (P1–P4)
- Credit Decision Score Framework
